MKUltra: CIA Mind Control Experiments

Documented CIA program involving unauthorized human experimentation.

Official Documentation

1977 Senate Hearing Transcripts

Church Committee hearings on intelligence activities

CINA Documents

Declassified CIA files on behavior modification

1975 Rockefeller Commission

Report on CIA activities within the United States

Verified Facts:

Tuskegee Syphilis Study

40-year unethical medical experiment on African American men.

Official Documentation

1973 Senate Hearing

"Human Experimentation" hearings

CDC Records

Public Health Service documents

1997 Presidential Apology

Official government apology

Verified Facts:

  • Study ran from 1932-1972
  • 600 impoverished African American men enrolled
  • Participants deceived about treatment
  • Penicillin withheld after it became available

Official Sources:

  • U.S. Public Health Service. "Tuskegee Syphilis Study Administrative Records"
  • National Archives. "Records of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention"
  • U.S. Senate Committee on Labor and Public Welfare (1973). "Quality of Health Care-Human Experimentation"
  • The Presidential Apology (1997) - White House Records
