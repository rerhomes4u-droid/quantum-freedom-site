EMERGENCY MENTAL HEALTH PROTOCOLS

PHASE 1: IMMEDIATE STABILIZATION (First 24 Hours)
  • Grounding Technique: Name 5 things you can see, 4 things you can touch, 3 things you can hear, 2 things you can smell, 1 thing you can taste
  • Breathing Protocol: 4-7-8 breathing (inhale 4 seconds, hold 7, exhale 8) for 5 cycles
  • Safety First: Remove immediate means of self-harm from your environment
  • Emergency Contact: Reach out to one trusted person immediately
  • Professional Help: Contact a crisis hotline or mental health professional
PHASE 2: CRISIS MANAGEMENT (Days 2-7)
  • Structured Routine: Create and follow a simple hourly schedule
  • Basic Self-Care: Focus on hydration, simple nutrition, and basic hygiene
  • Limited Exposure: Reduce sensory input and decision-making demands
  • Therapeutic Tools: Use provided emergency coping strategies regularly
  • Professional Follow-up: Schedule and attend mental health appointments
PHASE 3: STABILIZATION (Weeks 2-4)
  • Gradual Reintegration: Slowly increase activity and social contact
  • Prevention Planning: Develop a relapse prevention strategy
  • Support System: Build and utilize a network of support
  • Long-term Tools: Implement sustainable mental health practices
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor symptoms and recovery progress
