THE SEVEN HABITS: From Dependence to Interdependence

PRIVATE VICTORY: The Path to Independence
  • Habit 1 - Be Proactive: Take responsibility for your life. Recognize your power to choose your response to any circumstance.
  • Habit 2 - Begin with the End in Mind: Define your personal mission and values. Create a clear mental vision of your desired destination.
  • Habit 3 - Put First Things First: Prioritize and execute around your most important goals. Live by design, not by default.
PUBLIC VICTORY: The Path to Interdependence
  • Habit 4 - Think Win-Win: Foster mutual benefit in all interactions. See life as cooperative, not competitive.
  • Habit 5 - Seek First to Understand: Practice empathic listening. Understand others deeply before seeking to be understood.
  • Habit 6 - Synergize: Value differences and build on strengths. Create third-alternative solutions that are better than any original proposal.
RENEWAL: The Path to Continuous Improvement
  • Habit 7 - Sharpen the Saw: Preserve and enhance your greatest asset - yourself. Regularly renew the four dimensions of your nature.
  • The Upward Spiral: Growth is a continuous, upward spiral of learning, commitment, and doing.
  • Inside-Out: Change begins within ourselves first, then radiates outward to influence our relationships and organizations.
