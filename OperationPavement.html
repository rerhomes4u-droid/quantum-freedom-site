EXPERIMENTAL FORMULAS

These formulas create instant, durable repairs using common materials.

‚ö° FORMULA ALPHA: INSTANT ROCK

Ingredients:

  • Baking soda (sodium bicarbonate) - 1 cup
  • Cyanoacrylate (super glue) - 100ml
  • Sand (optional, for texture match) - 2 cups
  • Water (for pre-wetting) - 50ml

Procedure:

  1. Clean pothole thoroughly (wire brush)
  2. Wet surface with water spray
  3. Fill 3/4 with baking soda/sand mix
  4. Pour super glue evenly over surface
  5. Wait 30 seconds - chemical reaction creates instant solid
  6. Add second layer if needed
  7. Texture with asphalt dust for camouflage

üî• FORMULA BETA: COLD PATCH PLUS

Enhanced commercial cold patch formula:

  • Store-bought cold patch asphalt - 1 bag
  • Portland cement - 10% by weight
  • Epoxy resin (two-part) - 5% by weight
  • Crushed glass (for reflectivity) - handful

Enhancement makes permanent instead of temporary:

  1. Mix dry ingredients first
  2. Add epoxy resin and hardener
  3. Knead until uniform color
  4. Apply like normal cold patch
  5. Sets in 15 minutes, cures to concrete strength in 24h

üåÄ FORMULA GAMMA: RECYCLED RUBBER

Using recycled materials for eco-repair:

  • Shredded tire rubber - 2 cups
  • Asphalt emulsion - 1 cup
  • Bentonite clay (waterproofing) - 1/2 cup
  • Liquid nails adhesive - 1/4 cup

Flexible, waterproof, eco-friendly:

  1. Mix rubber and clay first
  2. Add asphalt emulsion slowly
  3. Mix in adhesive last
  4. Pack into hole firmly
  5. Creates flexible, waterproof patch
