Welcome to Deliverance Prayers

A collection of Catholic prayers for spiritual warfare and protection, approved for use by the laity.

This application provides easy access to the prayers contained in the book "Deliverance Prayers for Use by the Laity" published by Sensus Traditions Press.

According to the introduction: "The Church in her wisdom and experience has always known that authority is one of the primary requisites in order to drive a demon out. Since diabolic influence occurs in our bodies (and not in our souls), the laity may use prayers as long as they are not forbidden by the Church and which by their nature do not imply an authority one does not have."

Browse through the prayers using the navigation on the left, or use the search function to find specific prayers.
